Art and design

I craft, paint and design. Read my story!



My interest in art started with jewelry crafting, and I slowly developed my interest in brush calligraphy, watercolour art and resin art.






With this love for art and crafting, I started a small online business, Coxo Vanessa in 2011. Coxo Vanessa is run and managed individually, and is first launched on Instagram. Initially, Coxo Vanessa specialises in customised jewelry. However, as I developed my interest in different forms of art, vinyl and self-designed prints are introduced to the brand. Today, it operates on an online website and offers vinyl and card customisations, together with personalised jewelry.

Workshop

A jewelry making workshop held in Clementi Mall as part of The Clementi Fiesta. Participants were mostly children aged between 8-12 years old where they learnt how to craft their very own chain woven bracelet.
